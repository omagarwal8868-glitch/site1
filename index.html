About Govind Pansari

Since 1950, Govind Pansari has been Agra's most trusted name in premium dry fruits and authentic Indian spices. What started as a small shop in belanganj agra has grown into three thriving locations across the city, serving thousands of satisfied customers.


We pride ourselves on sourcing only the finest quality products directly from farms and trusted suppliers. Every almond, cashew, and spice that reaches you has been carefully selected, tested, and packaged to preserve its natural goodness. When you choose Govind Pansari, you choose purity, quality, and 75 years of trust.
